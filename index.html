Branding for Norda Stelo

Blue for corporate, orange for marketing.

Norda Stelo's corporate image is the result of solid team work. An amazing team I've been part of since 2011 and one that has evolved remarkably lately.

The logo and its guidelines were developed by the ridiculously talented Alex Rainville, my colleague at the firm. She did such a great job that it really is a piece of cake to create something nice out of it. These are some of the pieces that were under my belt, but her touch is undeniably present; we get along very well despite the distance; she's in Quebec City and I'm in Montreal, and yet, we talk everyday and share our impressions about almost everything that comes and goes related to the company's corporate image.

When you love what you do and you are lucky enough to be surrounded by the the right people, work doesn't really feel like so.
