About your privacy #

The purpose of this section is to explain the way I use and protect the data gathered during your visits. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy.

Note that the practices established in this privacy policy are for this website only. If you follow links to other websites, do review the privacy policies posted at those sites.

About your information, its protection and use #

This note applies to all information obtained or submitted on patimontero.ca. When you email me, I collect your email address. Though, I can erase it as per your request. I also use Google Analytics and Hotjar to get data related to age, sex, location and browsing behaviour.

I only use the information you provide about yourself to improve your experience. I do not share this information with outside parties.

I have set physical and electronic procedures to secure the information obtained.

I do not send communications for you to subscribe or unsubscribe.
