Cards #

Cards are composable components made out of a short text, a link and often, an image. Their main goal is to highlight key content to entice the users and take them to it.

Guidelines

  • Keep the content below five words to prevent text wrapping.
  • Use meaningful text that gives users an idea of where it's taking them. Avoid the use of vague sentences like Click here.
